Sea Otter vs Communist Zombies

Description:

Sea Otter vs Communist Zombies is a classic 2D platformer heavily focused on projectile and boss battles. Inspired by the 16-bit video games of the 90’s, the visuals and audio are carefully created to match the style of that era. Don’t miss out on this non-stop action adventure!

About The Game:

Defend freedom and democracy by throwing one seashell at a time! Play as NATO elite operative Sam the sea otter against hordes of communist zombies unleashed by ruthless bosses such as Rocket Kim, Xijiji, and Vladimir Pudding. Take your fight from the tropical Taiwan Strait all the way to Pyongyang, North Korea. Unlock new powerful weapons and discover hidden secrets while you try to save humanity from World War III.

Devil King Kong vs The World

Description:

Devil King Kong vs The World is a story game where your main mission is to save the people of the world from the wrath of Devil King Kong. Working together with a team or on your own you will be taken on a story mission where you are in the year 2035 and Devil King Kong has just made his entrance to earth from his home planet sigma. Gear up and prepare yourself for daunting tasks, intense battles, and a world full of chaos.

About The Game:

You are a past military professional who was a part of the Beekeeper program, a top secret branch of the government known for their tactical and combat skills. After being retired for 10+ years you are called back up to the program for an urgent matter. You are tasked with helping fight off King Kong, a well known enemy on earth for over the past 50 years.
